Why Space Exploration Matters

Space exploration has transformed our understanding of the universe and our place in it. From discovering new planets and galaxies to developing life-saving technologies, the benefits of space exploration are immense.

Recent Space Discovery: Evidence of Water on Mars

Scientists have found evidence of liquid water on Mars, increasing the chances of finding life on the red planet. This discovery was made possible by the Mars rover Curiosity, which has been exploring the planet since 2012.
