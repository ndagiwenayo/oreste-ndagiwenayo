Scientists discover new exoplanet

A team of astronomers have discovered a new exoplanet that is similar in size and composition to Earth. The planet, named Proxima Centauri b, is located in the habitable zone of its star, and may be able to support life.

NASA launches new mission to study the sun

NASA has launched a new spacecraft, called Solar Probe Plus, to study the sun up close. The mission will provide valuable information about the sun's atmosphere and help scientists understand the solar wind, which can have an impact on Earth's magnetic field and climate.

China becomes first country to land on the far side of the moon

China's Chang'e-4 spacecraft has successfully landed on the far side of the moon, becoming the first country to do so. The mission will explore the uncharted terrain on the far side of the moon and conduct experiments on the lunar surface.
